About us

Enamel is a company with a group of artists who craft unique colorful designs on jewellery using enamel.

Enamelling is a colorful way to decorate objects. It involves the fusing of glass by heat onto a metal surface. Metals used for enamelling include gold, silver, copper, stainless steel, and platinum.

Every enamel creation is an original work of art.

Quality

To protect your enamel jewelery from getting damaged and scratched, store your enamel jewelery in a soft and clean cloth bag.

To clean your enamel jewelery, use warm water, and a soft, clean and dry cloth to dry your enamel jewelry. Be sure to remove any soap residue. Enamel paint is also waterproof, making it perfectly suitable for any outdoor condition.

Avoid direct sunlight, and the high temperature on your enamel jewelery if possible.
